Sustaino is an Online store to provide eco-friendly customized products.

The World Bank released a report predicting that global annual waste generation will

increase from 2.01 billion tonnes in 2016 to 3.5 billion tonnes in 2050.

The report estimates that the treatment and disposal of waste contributed to five

percent of global emissions in 2016.

Sustaino promots to increase the use of recycled products to reduce the amount of

waste thereby helping our earth to become more eco-friendly.
